### [AI Content Orchestrator](/products/ai-content-orchestrator/) — Free on WordPress.org

Not just another AI writer — a complete orchestration layer.

Edit in WordPress, publish to Jekyll on GitHub Pages. Style-aware Markdown conversion that reads your existing Jekyll site to match its conventions, GitHub OAuth (no token pasting), two-way sync, inline + featured image upload, internal link rewriting, and optional AI for SEO descriptions and image alt text via Claude or OpenAI. **Coming soon to WordPress.org.**
