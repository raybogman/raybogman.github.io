## Stop writing content. Start orchestrating it.

Every AI writing tool on the market does the same thing: you type a prompt, it spits out text. Then you're left with the same problems you had before — no strategy, no research, no editorial flow, no quality control, and a WordPress editor full of copy-paste.

**AI Content Orchestrator** is different. It doesn't just write — it manages the **entire content pipeline** inside WordPress, from first research to published post.

---

## What it does

### Research & strategy
- **Topic discovery** — surface content opportunities based on your niche, competitors, and audience intent
- **Keyword research** — built-in SERP analysis and keyword clustering, no separate tools needed
- **Content briefs** — auto-generated briefs with target keywords, audience, tone, structure, and competitive gaps

### Planning & structure
- **Editorial calendar** — plan, schedule, and manage your content pipeline visually
- **Outline generation** — AI-assisted outlines based on the brief, SERP data, and your brand guidelines
- **Content scoring** — evaluate outlines before writing starts, not after

### Writing & editing
- **AI-assisted drafting** — generates content that follows the brief, not random AI slop
- **Brand voice control** — configure tone, style, vocabulary, and guardrails so output sounds like you
- **Built-in editing** — readability scoring, grammar, SEO optimization, and fact-check flags
- **Human-in-the-loop** — AI drafts, you approve. No auto-publishing unless you want it

### Publishing & beyond
- **Direct to WordPress** — posts, pages, custom post types. No copy-paste
- **Scheduled publishing** — set it and forget it, or drip content on a cadence
- **Performance tracking** — see what's working after publication, feed insights back into the next brief
- **Bulk operations** — orchestrate 10 or 100 pieces at once with the same quality controls

## FAQ

### Is this just another AI writing plugin?
No. Most AI plugins start and end at "generate text." AI Content Orchestrator manages the full pipeline — research, briefs, outlines, drafting, editing, and publishing — so every piece has strategy behind it before a single word is written.

### Which AI models does it use?
It's model-flexible — works with leading AI providers so you're never locked into a single vendor. You choose the model that fits your quality and cost needs.

### Does it auto-publish content?
Only if you want it to. By default, every piece goes through a human-in-the-loop approval step before publishing. You control the level of automation.

### Does it work with any WordPress theme?
Yes. It outputs standard WordPress posts, pages, and custom post types. Your theme handles the frontend — AI Content Orchestrator handles the pipeline.

### Can I use it for client sites?
Yes. The Business and Agency plans support multiple sites. Manage all content from one dashboard.

### What if I already use Yoast / RankMath / other SEO plugins?
They complement each other. AI Content Orchestrator handles the content pipeline; your SEO plugin handles on-page optimization, schema, sitemaps, etc.
